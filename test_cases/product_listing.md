 1 Product Listing Page - Test Cases

TC-PLP-001 — Verify products load successfully

    Precondition: User is logged in and on the product listing page 

    Steps:
    - Navigate to the product listing page 
    - Observe the product grid 

    Expected Result:
    - Page loads without errors 
    - At least one product card is visible 



TC-PLP-002 — Verify product details are displayed

    Precondition: Product listing page is loaded 

    Steps:
    - Observe the first product card 
   
    Expected Result:
    - Product name is visible 
    -Product price is visible 
    -“Add to Cart” button is enabled 



TC-PLP-003 — Verify sorting by price (Low → High)

    Steps:
    - Select “Price: Low to High” sorting 
    - Observe product prices 

    Expected Result:
    -Products are displayed in ascending price order

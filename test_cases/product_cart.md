3 Product Cart - Test Cases

TC-CART-001 — Verify product is added to cart

    Steps:
    - Add product to cart 
    - Open cart page 

    Expected Result:
    - Product appears in cart 
    - Quantity is correct 


TC-CART-002 — Verify cart total calculation

    Steps:
    - Add 2 quantities of product 
    - Observe line total and subtotal 

    Expected Result:
    - Line total = price × quantity 
    - Subtotal is accurate 


TC-CART-003 — Verify removing product updates totals

    Steps:
    - Remove product from cart

    Expected Result:
    - Product removed 
    - Cart subtotal updated 
    - Empty cart message shown (if applicable)
